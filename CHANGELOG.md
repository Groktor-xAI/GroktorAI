# What's New in Groktor

**Your AI-Powered Health Assistant**

---

## Features

### Interactive Body Map
Simply tap on any body part to quickly describe where you're experiencing symptoms. No more struggling to explain your discomfort - just point and click!

### Severity Meter
Get visual feedback on how concerning your symptoms might be with our easy-to-understand 1-5 scale indicator, ranging from green (low concern) to red (seek immediate care).

### First Aid Guides
Quick reference guides for 6 common emergencies:
- **Burns** - Heat, chemical, and sunlight burns
- **Cuts & Wounds** - Minor cuts, scrapes, and lacerations
- **Choking** - Emergency airway obstruction response
- **Allergic Reactions** - Mild to severe reactions and anaphylaxis
- **Sprains & Strains** - Twisted ankles and pulled muscles
- **Nosebleeds** - How to stop and manage common nosebleeds

Each guide includes:
- Step-by-step instructions
- When to seek medical help
- What NOT to do

### Find Nearby Care
One tap to locate medical facilities near you using your device's location.

### Chat History
Your conversations are saved so you can revisit past health discussions anytime. Access your history from the sidebar.

### Smooth Animations
A polished, modern experience with beautiful transitions powered by Framer Motion.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI
- **Backend:** Express.js, Node.js
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Neon Serverless)
- **AI:** OpenRouter (Grok model)
- **Animations:** Framer Motion
