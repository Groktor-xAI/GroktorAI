Get fresh health tips every day to support your wellbeing. Tips can be refreshed or dismissed as needed.

Groktor is now fully translated in 11 languages:

- Spanish (Español)
- French (Français)
- German (Deutsch)
- Chinese (中文)
- Japanese (日本語)
- Korean (한국어)
- Russian (Русский)
- Portuguese (Português)
- Hindi (हिन्दी)
- Arabic (العربية)

## Voice Input

Speak your symptoms instead of typing. Simply tap the microphone button to start voice recognition (available in Chrome and Edge browsers).
